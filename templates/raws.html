Raws — Calculated Columns Guide
Physical Quantities
ColumnHow it's calculated
Long Physical Qty (Lots)Physical long quantity converted into futures-equivalent lots:
= ROUND(Qty Long ÷ 50.8, 0)
Short Physical Qty (Lots)Physical short quantity converted into futures-equivalent lots:
= ROUND(−Qty Short ÷ 50.8, 0)
PolPolarisation factor applied in SB-linked price conversion via (1 + Pol):
• Uses actual Pol if provided
• Defaults to 0.0375 for Washout
• Defaults to 0.042 otherwise
Affects both pricing and exposure calculations.
Hedge Positions
ColumnHow it's calculated
Purchase HedgesNet futures position linked to AGP (non-Pol trades only), sourced from Trades.
Sales HedgesNet futures position linked to AGS (non-Pol trades only), sourced from Trades.
Purchase HedgedTrue if:
|Purchase Hedges (lots)| = |Long Physical Qty (lots)|
Sales HedgedTrue if:
|Sales Hedges (lots)| = |Short Physical Qty (lots)|
Purchase Pol HedgeNet futures position linked to AGP (Pol trades only), sourced from Trades.
Sales Pol HedgeNet futures position linked to AGS (Pol trades only), sourced from Trades.
Options DeltaNet delta exposure from options positions (AGP + AGS), sourced from Trades. Represents the effective futures-equivalent exposure after delta adjustment.
Settlement Prices
ColumnHow it's calculated
Purchase SettlementFutures settlement price for purchase contract (from price table or RTD).
Sales SettlementFutures settlement price for sales contract (from price table or RTD).
Pricing Status
ColumnHow it's calculated
Purchase StatusCompares |Hedges (lots)| vs |Physical Qty (lots)|. All comparisons in lots, not MT.
Basis trades:
• Hedges = 0 → Unpriced
• Hedges < Qty → Partially Priced
• Hedges = Qty → Fully Priced
• Hedges > Qty → Over Priced
Flat trades:
• Hedges = 0 → Unhedged
• Hedges < Qty → Partially Hedged
• Hedges = Qty → Fully Hedged
• Hedges > Qty → Over Hedged
Sales StatusSame logic as Purchase Status, comparing Sales Hedges vs Short Physical Qty.
Special rule: returns "Delivered" if AGS = Delivered (takes priority over all other logic).
Futures Pricing Logic
ColumnHow it's calculated
Purchase Futures PricingFully Hedged: Total Hedge Cost ÷ Hedge Lots
Partially Hedged: (Hedged Portion + Unhedged Portion × Settlement) ÷ Total Qty
Unhedged: Settlement Price
Sales Futures PricingSame logic applied to sales side.
Basis
ColumnHow it's calculated
Purchase BasisBasis trades: input basis value
Flat trades: derived: Purchase Input − Purchase Futures Pricing
Sales BasisBasis trades: input basis value
Flat trades: derived: Sales Input − Sales Futures Pricing
Prices
ColumnHow it's calculated
Purchase PriceBasis (¢/lb): (FP + Basis) × 22.0462 × (1 + Pol)
Basis ($/MT): (FP × 22.0462) + Basis
Flat trades: Input Price
If Incoterm = FCA: subtract Elevation
Sales PriceSame structure as Purchase Price.
Purchase Cost (CNF)Purchase Price + Freight + Insurance + Financing + Misc
Polarisation
ColumnHow it's calculated
Long Physical PolLong Physical Qty × Pol factor, adjusted by pricing status and units.
Short Physical Pol−(Short Physical Qty × Pol factor), adjusted by pricing status.
Pol ExposureNet polarisation exposure:
Long Physical Pol + Short Physical Pol + Purchase Pol Hedge + Sales Pol Hedge
Represents residual exposure due to quality (pol) mismatch after hedging.
Exposure
ColumnHow it's calculated
Long Physical ExposureBasis trades:
• If Over Priced → Long Physical Qty
• Otherwise → −Purchase Hedges
Flat trades:
• Always Long Physical Qty
Short Physical ExposureBasis trades:
• If Over Priced → Short Physical Qty
• Otherwise → −Sales Hedges
Flat trades:
• Always Short Physical Qty
Purchase ExposureExposure contributed by the purchase leg:
Basis trades: only contributes when over-priced
Flat trades: full physical + hedge exposure
Sales ExposureSame logic as Purchase Exposure, applied to the sales leg (AGS side).
Overall ExposureNet exposure expressed in futures-equivalent lots:
= Purchase Exposure + Sales Exposure + Pol Exposure + Options Delta
Positive → net long | Negative → net short
Spreads
ColumnHow it's calculated
SpreadsNet spread position from Trades (AGP + AGS). Uses Trades[Spread Position] (not Trades[Position]). Excludes "Pol" trades. Represents calendar or inter-contract spreads.
Spread ValueAverage price per spread lot:
= Total Spread PnL ÷ Spread Lots
Sign determined by net spread position direction.
Spread ExposureResidual exposure after accounting for spreads:
If purchase ≠ sales contract: Long Physical Qty − abs(Spreads)
If same contract: −abs(Spreads)
PnL
ColumnHow it's calculated
Physical PNL(Sales Price − Purchase Cost CNF) × max(Qty Long, Qty Short)
Uses larger leg to reflect actual exposure.
Futures PNLNet PnL from Trades (AGP + AGS):
• Includes realised and mark-to-market PnL
• Includes all futures trades (Pol and non-Pol)
• Already aggregated at source
Total PNLPhysical PNL + Futures PNL
